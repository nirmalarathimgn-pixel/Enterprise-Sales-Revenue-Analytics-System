# 📊 Enterprise Sales & Revenue Analytics System

## 🚀 Project Overview

The Enterprise Sales & Revenue Analytics System is an end-to-end business intelligence project designed to transform raw sales data into actionable insights.

Using SQL, Python, and Power BI, this project analyzes sales performance, customer behavior, product profitability, and regional trends to support strategic business decision-making.

The solution follows a complete analytics workflow from data cleaning and exploration to KPI reporting, forecasting, and executive dashboarding.

---

## 🎯 Business Objective

Organizations often collect large volumes of sales data but struggle to answer critical business questions:

- Which products generate the highest revenue?
- Which customers contribute the most profit?
- Which regions perform best?
- How are sales trending over time?
- What actions can improve future business performance?

This project provides a centralized analytics solution to answer these questions through data-driven insights.
